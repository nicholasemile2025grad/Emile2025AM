Lobster Mushroom

Hypomyces lactifluorum

The Lobster Mushroom is not a mushroom species by itself, but a parasitic fungus (Hypomyces lactifluorum) that grows on certain gilled mushrooms, turning them bright orange-red and giving them a firm texture and unique flavor.

Genus: Hypomyces

Species: lactifluorum

Type: Gilled Mushroom (parasitized)

Common Names: Lobster Mushroom
